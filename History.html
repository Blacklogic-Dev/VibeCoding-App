1. Birth of AI

The concept of Artificial Intelligence (AI) dates back to the 1950s when Alan Turing posed the question "Can machines think?" This laid the foundation for decades of research into mimicking human intelligence.

2. AI Milestones

Through the 60s to early 2000s, developments in neural networks, expert systems, and machine learning shaped AI into a practical tool for automation and decision-making.

3. Coding Revolution

As software development grew, so did the need for languages like Python, Java, and JavaScript—laying the backbone for full-stack development and logic-driven systems.

4. Rise of Prompt Engineering

With the birth of large language models like GPT and Gemini, prompt engineering emerged as a new skill—crafting precise inputs to get valuable outputs from AI models.

5. Fusion of Coding and Prompting

Developers began blending AI-generated code with their own logic using prompt engineering. This gave birth to a new movement: VibeCoding — a fusion of creativity, tech, and AI collaboration.

6. Creation of VibeCoding

Founded by Professor BlackLogic and Queen Asanda, VibeCoding is a modern intelligence school focused on mastering prompt engineering, coding, and AI innovation in a way that’s fun, fast, and future-ready.
